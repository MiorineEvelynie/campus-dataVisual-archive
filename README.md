# Automotive Sales & Profitability Analytics

## Overview
This repository contains a Tableau visual analytics project designed to assist auction managers and pricing strategists in the automotive industry[cite: 2]. The project evaluates the sales performance of 446,806 vehicles from January to July 2015 to identify pricing inefficiencies by comparing selling prices to the Manheim Market Report (MMR) valuations[cite: 2].

## Dashboards Overview
The analysis is separated into two coordinated dashboards to avoid cognitive overload and provide deep analytical insights[cite: 2]:

*   **Dashboard 1: Exploratory Analysis** 
    Analyzes market behavior trends, including the impact of odometer readings on selling prices, the top 10 car brands, and monthly sales volume compositions based on MMR valuation status[cite: 2].
*   **Dashboard 2: Profitability Assessment** 
    Focuses on the analytical claim that high sales volumes are masking widespread below-market transactions[cite: 2]. It features a diverging bar chart showing average margins versus MMR across vehicle makes, price distributions using box plots, and monthly condition compositions[cite: 2].

## Key Insights
*   **Pricing Inefficiency:** Despite high sales volumes and a 66.46% ratio of Prime and Good condition vehicles, many transactions fell below fair market value (MMR)[cite: 2]. 
*   **Margin Deficits:** The margin analysis across brands reveals widespread pricing inefficiencies, with only two brands (Hummer and Suzuki) maintaining positive average margins against the MMR[cite: 2].
*   **Value Retention:** Prime condition vehicles consistently maintain better resale value over time compared to lower condition tiers[cite: 2].
