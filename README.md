# Automotive Sales & Profitability Analytics

## Overview
A tableau visual analytics project that is meant to help auction managers and pricing analysts in the automobile sector. The project will analyze the sales performance of 446,806 cars between January and July 2015 in order to uncover pricing inefficiencies, as well as compare the selling price with MMR valuations.

## Dashboards Overview
The analysis is separated into two coordinated dashboards to avoid cognitive overload and provide deep analytical insights:

*   **Dashboard 1: Exploratory Analysis** 
    Analyzes market behavior trends, including the impact of odometer readings on selling prices, the top 10 car brands, and monthly sales volume compositions based on MMR valuation status.
*   **Dashboard 2: Profitability Assessment** 
    Focuses on the analytical claim that high sales volumes are masking widespread below-market transactions. It features a diverging bar chart showing average margins versus MMR across vehicle makes, price distributions using box plots, and monthly condition compositions.

## Key Insights
*   **Pricing Inefficiency:** Despite high sales volumes and a 66.46% ratio of Prime and Good condition vehicles, many transactions fell below fair market value (MMR). 
*   **Margin Deficits:** The margin analysis across brands reveals widespread pricing inefficiencies, with only two brands (Hummer and Suzuki) maintaining positive average margins against the MMR.
*   **Value Retention:** Prime condition vehicles consistently maintain better resale value over time compared to lower condition tiers.
